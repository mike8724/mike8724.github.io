About the Project

The aim of this project is to explore the methods and Techniques of Computer Aided Design. This project will design an artifact and carry out a critical analysis on CAD software packages.

Analysis of CAD software.


For this project, Fusion 360 was selected for its ease of use and cost structure offering a student license.

Fusion 360 is an Autodesk product which offers some powerful features. When stacked up against other packages, its easy to see why people prefer it for personal and small business use.

I personally have no useful experience in CAD. But to make a gear which could be used on a robot chassis or for an intricate joint took less than 30 minutes after a little familiarity and instruction.
