About the Module

The module enabled me to design and manufacture models in a variety of media using state of the art technology. From Human head design, limbs and Android skin design, I will find this useful for converting your virtual designs into a physical form. The module will also enable me to gain understanding, apply knowledge, analyse and evaluate problems and create solutions through a variety of activities, including laboratories where teaching of software, demonstrations, practical exercises will be undertaken.

Skills

Core Skills Used

Design

Using a digital clay CAD package for virtual prototypes

3D Scanning

The use of 3D laser scanning and coordinate measurement techniques to capture physical models into the virtual design environments.

Skin Production

Resin and latex production using standard production robotic skin techniques with post-production methods for aesthetic covering e.g., Shading skin using Paint etc.

Material Manufacture

Using 3D printers, Lasercutting and Resin printers in a variety of material to construct simple mechanical joint construction.
